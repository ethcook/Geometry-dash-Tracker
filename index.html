How to Use Geometry Dash Tracker

What this site is for

This tracker helps you improve at Geometry Dash by keeping a record of your goals, demons, practice sessions, and weaknesses.

How to use it

  • Add goals to stay focused on what you want to beat next.
  • Log demons you beat with difficulty, attempts, and notes.
  • Track practice sessions to see how much time you spend improving.
  • Mark your weak mechanics to focus on the sections you need to practice.
  • Visit the dashboard for quick stats and the timeline for progress over time.

About the website

This tool is designed for Geometry Dash players who want a simple, local tracker for practice, demons, and improvement goals. Your data stays in your browser.
